Professional standards

Compliance & Regulation.

The professional framework within which the practice operates.
RICS-regulated, independently insured, properly governed.

01 · Regulation
RICS membership and standards.

RICS Regulation.

RH Chartered Surveyors is regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS). Rob Haigh has been a Member since 2014, and the practice operates in full accordance with the RICS Rules of Conduct.

As a RICS-regulated firm, RH Chartered Surveyors is required to operate within a defined professional and ethical framework, acting with integrity and independence at all times. This matters because it sets a clear standard for how I work. Advice is objective, independent, and properly considered.

RICS membership provides clients with assurance that they are working with a properly qualified, accountable professional operating to recognised industry standards. All advice is provided objectively and in the best interests of the client.

02 · Complaints
If something isn't right.

Complaints Handling.

If you have a concern about any aspect of the service provided, please raise it with me directly in the first instance. All complaints are taken seriously and reviewed carefully.

If a complaint cannot be resolved internally, access to an independent redress mechanism is available as required by RICS. The full complaints handling procedure can be read here.

03 · Insurance
Professional indemnity cover.

Professional Indemnity Insurance.

RH Chartered Surveyors maintains professional indemnity insurance in accordance with RICS requirements. This provides protection for both the firm and its clients in the event that a claim arises in connection with professional advice or services provided.

Details of the firm's insurance arrangements are available on request.

04 · AML / KYC
Identity verification.

Anti-Money Laundering.

RH Chartered Surveyors is required to comply with the Money Laundering Regulations 2017 (as amended). Before commencing instructions, identity verification and beneficial ownership checks are conducted on clients in line with RICS guidance.

This is standard practice across the industry and exists to protect the integrity of UK property transactions. Information provided for AML purposes is handled in accordance with the practice's data protection obligations.

05 · Conflicts
How independence is preserved.

Conflicts of Interest.

Before accepting any instruction, conflicts of interest are checked against the practice's existing client list. Where a potential or actual conflict is identified, the matter is declined or, where appropriate, disclosed in writing to all relevant parties before proceeding.

This applies particularly to acquisition and disposal work, where the practice does not act for both sides of any transaction. Independent advice means independent throughout.

06 · Data
Privacy & data protection.

Data Protection.

The practice processes personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Personal data is collected only where necessary for the conduct of professional services or compliance with regulatory obligations.

A full statement on what data is collected, how it is used, and the rights you hold over your data is set out in the Privacy Policy.

Redress Scheme.

Details of RH Chartered Surveyors' registered redress scheme will be published here shortly. In the interim, please refer to the complaints handling procedure above for details of internal complaints handling and the independent redress mechanism required by RICS.
