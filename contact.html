What happens next
A conversation, first.

Conversation first. No obligation.

The first conversation is exactly that. A conversation. You tell me what you're working on or wrestling with, and I'll tell you whether I think I can help. There's no formal pitch, no engagement letter at the end of the call, no obligation to take it any further.

If it's a fit, we'll work out scope and fees together before any work starts. If it isn't, I'll tell you that plainly. Sometimes I'll point you to someone better placed to help, or suggest the matter doesn't actually need a surveyor at all.

Send me whatever you've got. A lease, a proposal, a rateable value, a screenshot of a difficult email. Anything that helps me understand the situation before we speak. I'll read it before the call so the conversation is useful from the first minute.
