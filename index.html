About Rob

The person on the phone is the person doing the work. Always.

I've been practising as a Chartered Surveyor since 2012, working across both the private and public sectors with some of the UK's best-known property firms. I established RH to do things differently — to act for clients directly, across every property matter they face, without handovers or layers in between.

"I invest the time to understand your business, your objectives, and your risk profile. Advice is aligned with long-term goals, not just the immediate issue."

Clients range from local businesses through to property companies, high net worth individuals, and portfolio landlords. What they share is a need for advice that looks beyond a single transaction and considers the wider commercial picture.
