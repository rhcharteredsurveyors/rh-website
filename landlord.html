The portfolio reality
A constant flow of decisions.

A portfolio generates a constant flow of property matters — and most of them intersect.

  • Lease coming up for renewal
  • Vacancy to let
  • Asset to position for sale
  • Acquisition on the table
  • Tenant looking to break or assign
  • Dispute to settle before it escalates

What you do with one affects your position on the next. Which is why landlords come to me for the breadth as much as the depth.

I'm a general practice chartered surveyor, advising across every discipline and every commercial sector — office, industrial, retail, leisure, mixed-use. Lease events, acquisitions, disposals, management, valuation, rates, disputes. One relationship across all of it.

Some landlords instruct me for a specific job. Others have me as their lead advisor across an entire portfolio. The structure scales to what you need — and either way, the relationship is the same.
