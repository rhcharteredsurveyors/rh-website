Case Study · Long-Term Management

Larkfield, Mattersey.

Reorganised an informal verbal occupation into clean multi-tenant industrial leases. Retained for over six years as the client's principal property contact across the site.

At a glance
Key facts & figures.
  • Three industrial units at Larkfield, Mattersey, Doncaster
  • Lease restructuring across the site
  • New occupational leases for multiple tenants
  • Clear demises and shared access arrangements
  • Ongoing client instruction for over six years
  • Significant ongoing risk reduction for landlord
01 · Overview
The instruction context.

The instruction.

I was instructed by a private landlord to provide advice in relation to Units 1–3 Larkfield, Mattersey, a small industrial site located adjacent to the client's residential property.

Prior to my involvement, the premises were occupied by a single tenant under an informal verbal arrangement, with no clear lease structure in place and limited clarity over demises, shared areas or rights of access across the site.

The client had limited experience of commercial property matters and required professional support to regularise the occupational arrangements and protect the long-term position of the asset.

02 · Our role
What I did.

What the work involved.

I advised the client on a strategy to restructure the occupational arrangements across the site.

Following the departure of the existing occupier, I negotiated a new lease for Unit 1, and subsequently secured separate leases for Units 2 and 3 with new tenants.

A key part of the role was ensuring that all parties clearly understood the agreed lease terms, with careful consideration given to the demises of each unit, the shared areas across the site, and rights of access required for day-to-day operation.

This structured approach ensured that the site could function effectively with multiple occupiers operating alongside one another.

03 · Outcome
What the client got.

The outcome.

The site is now fully let under properly documented lease arrangements, providing the client with a stable and manageable income producing asset.

I have remained instructed for over six years, acting as the client's principal point of contact for all property matters relating to the site.

The clear structure established at the outset has ensured the units operate smoothly with minimal ongoing management requirements, demonstrating how proactive property management can significantly reduce long-term risk and involvement for landlords.
