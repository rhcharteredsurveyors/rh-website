What this work involves
Quiet work, properly done.

Property management done well is quiet work.

You're clear on what's happening with your asset. Your tenants feel properly looked after. Issues get handled before they escalate. You're free to think about things that actually need your attention.

I act as a professional intermediary between you and your tenants.

You stay in control of the decisions that matter. I handle the daily running. Tenants know exactly who to ring when something comes up, and they get a chartered surveyor on the line who can actually help.

What's included

The full management remit.

  • Tenant liaison as the single point of contact.
  • Periodic inspections with clear written reporting.
  • Preparing assets for lease events, refinancing, or disposal.
  • Monitoring lease obligations and flagging early issues.
  • Service charge management, administration, and budgeting.
  • Oversight of repairs, compliance, planned maintenance, and contractors.

How I approach it.

Six principles that shape every property management instruction, from new acquisitions to long-running portfolios.

01
Proactive, not reactive.
Issues flagged early, problems headed off before they become expensive. The cheapest repair is the one done in time.
02
Consistent contact.
Tenants deal with me throughout. Relationships build. Problems get smaller. No call centres, no rotating account managers.
03
A chartered surveyor on the line.
Most managing agents aren't chartered surveyors. Your tenants get someone with the qualification, experience and judgement to actually help.
04
Inspections that mean something.
Site visits with proper observation and written reporting. Not box-ticking, not glancing in the window. You know the state of your asset.
05
You're always in the picture.
Clear written updates at a cadence that suits you. The things you actually need to know, in the format that works for you.
06
Escalation when it matters.
Routine matters I handle. Anything affecting value, income, or legal exposure comes to you with a recommendation, not just a question.
An honest position

Right person, right job.

Property management and rent collection are different specialisms. I handle the property, day-to-day. Your accountant or bookkeeper handles the money.

Cleaner audit trails, no conflict of interest, and the right person doing the right job. I work alongside whoever handles your money side, supporting arrears recovery wherever it's needed.
