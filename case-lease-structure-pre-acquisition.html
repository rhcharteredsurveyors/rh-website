Case Study · Pre-Acquisition

Lease Structure Pre-Acquisition.

Reviewed and restructured the lease on an £820,000 retail investment before completion, securing additional term certain and removing a near-term break option to strengthen the income profile.

At a glance
Key facts & figures.
  • £68,000 per annum rental income
  • £820,000 purchase price
  • 8.3% gross initial yield (post-adjustment)
  • Retail investment
  • Lease terms revised prior to completion
  • Improved income security and reduced short-term risk
01 · Overview
The instruction context.

The instruction.

I advised a private investor on the proposed acquisition of a retail investment property producing £68,000 per annum.

While the asset offered an attractive headline return, the existing lease structure presented risks around short-term income continuity and tenant flexibility.

02 · Our role
What I did.

What the work involved.

A detailed review of the lease terms identified risks around term certain, break provisions and the timing of future lease events.

I advised the client on the commercial implications of the existing structure and engaged with the seller to agree revisions to the lease prior to completion.

This included securing additional term certain and removing a near-term tenant break option, strengthening the overall income profile. I advised throughout the transaction, ensuring the revised position was properly documented through to completion.

03 · Outcome
What the client got.

The outcome.

The acquisition completed at £820,000, reflecting a gross initial yield of approximately 8.3% following adjustment.

With the revised lease structure in place, the client secured a stronger and more predictable income stream, reducing short-term risk and improving the overall investment position.
