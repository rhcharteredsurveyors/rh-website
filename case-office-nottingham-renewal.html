Case Study · Multi-Stage Tenant

Office Tenant, Nottingham.

Acted across both rent review and subsequent lease renewal for the same tenant across three industrial units. Total savings in excess of £135,000, freeing up capital for investment in new machinery.

At a glance
Key facts & figures.
  • Three industrial units in Nottingham
  • Initial passing rent: £61,750 per annum
  • Landlord proposal: £102,300 per annum
  • Rent review settled at: £85,575 per annum
  • Renewal agreed at £101,600 with 6 months rent free
  • Total saving in excess of £135,000
01 · Overview
The instruction context.

The instruction.

I was instructed by an office tenant in 2020 to advise on a rent review across three units at Robin Hood Industrial Estate, Nottingham.

The landlord proposed a significant increase in rent from £61,750 to £102,300 per annum, creating immediate pressure on the business.

Following the success of this instruction, I was subsequently re-instructed to advise on the lease renewal five years later.

02 · Our role
What I did.

What the work involved.

At rent review, I advised the client on their position and the likely course of negotiations, recognising that the landlord, a large institutional investor, would take a firm stance. Negotiations were protracted, but I applied a clear strategy, including the credible threat of third-party determination, to apply pressure and drive movement. This resulted in a settlement at £85,575 per annum, significantly below the landlord's proposal.

Ahead of lease expiry, I took a proactive approach by serving a Section 26 Notice, securing continuation of the tenancy and effectively holding the existing rent for a further 12 months while negotiations progressed.

During renewal negotiations, the landlord initially proposed a rent of £105,000 per annum. I negotiated firmly on the tenant's behalf, ultimately agreeing terms at £101,600 per annum with a six-month rent-free period to offset the increase.

03 · Outcome
What the client got.

The outcome.

Across both instructions, the total saving secured for the client was in excess of £135,000.

The rent review outcome reduced the immediate financial impact of the landlord's proposal, while the lease renewal structure provided additional value through the agreed rent-free period.

This enabled the client to move forward with confidence, including investing in new machinery to support the growth and operational capacity of the business.
